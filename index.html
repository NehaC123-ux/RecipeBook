A Culinary Journey Through Flavors

Welcome to the Recipe Book, where culinary art meets creativity. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, this book offers a delightful mix of traditional recipes, modern twists, and everything in between.

Jeera Aloo

For Jeera Aloo, heat oil in a pan, then add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Sauté chopped potatoes until they start to soften. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt, and cook until the potatoes are tender. Stir in freshly chopped coriander and a squeeze of lemon juice for added flavor. Serve hot as a side dish with roti or rice.

Classic Margherita Pizza

For Classic Margherita Pizza, roll out pizza dough and place it on a baking sheet. Spread tomato sauce, add torn mozzarella, and top with fresh basil. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Bake at 475°F (245°C) for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted. Enjoy!

Paneer Butter Masala

For a delicious Paneer Butter Masala, start by heating oil and butter in a pan and adding cumin seeds. Sauté finely chopped onions until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a couple of minutes. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder, followed by tomato puree. Cook until the mixture thickens and the oil separates. Add cashew paste and cook for a few more minutes.

Chocolate Lava Cake

For Chocolate Lava Cake, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour ramekins. Melt 1/2 cup of butter with 1 cup of chopped chocolate in a microwave or double boiler. Whisk in 1 cup of powdered sugar and 2 large eggs, plus 2 egg yolks. Stir in 1/2 cup of flour until just combined. Pour the batter into the prepared ramekins.
